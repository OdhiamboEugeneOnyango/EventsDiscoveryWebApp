Community Guidelines

Safety Guidelines

Respect & Inclusion

Treat all community members with respect regardless of background, identity, or beliefs. Harassment, discrimination, and hate speech are not tolerated.

Personal Safety

Stay aware of your surroundings, travel in groups when possible, and report suspicious activities immediately. Trust your instincts if something feels wrong.

Emergency Procedures

Know the location of emergency exits and contact information. In case of emergency, prioritize your safety and call appropriate emergency services.

Reporting

Report incidents promptly and accurately. Your reports help keep the community safe. Anonymous reporting is available for sensitive situations.

Venue Guidelines

Operating Hours

Respect venue operating hours and capacity limits. Arrive and depart at appropriate times to maintain good community relationships.

Noise Levels

Be mindful of noise levels, especially in residential areas. Follow local noise ordinances and be considerate of neighbors.
